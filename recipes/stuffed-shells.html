Description

This is a recipe from The Gracious Wife for 5 Cheese Stuffed Shells. This is a meatless recipe for vegetarians. It takes 20 minutes to prepare, 35 minutes to cook, and serves 10. If you love cheese, this recipe is for you.

Ingredients

  • 20 jumbo pasta shells
  • 1 TBSP olive oil
  • 3-4 garlic cloves
  • 4 cups coarsely chopped spinach
  • one 12 oz container of ricotta
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded Romano cheese
  • 4 slices provolone cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • one 24 oz jar spaghetti sauce

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add garlic and spinach to skillet and saute until spinach is wilted and garlic is fragrant.
  3. In a large bowl, add ricotta and egg. Whisk together until combined.
  4. Add spinach mixture, cheeses, and Italian seasoning and stir to combine.
  5. Scoop about 2 tablespoons of the cheese mixture, and stuff into each shell.
  6. Pour 1/2 cup spaghetti sauce into the bottom of a 9"x13" baking dish and spread evenly.
  7. Place stuffed shells in the dish and cover with remaining sauce.
  8. Bake covered at 375 for 25 minutes.
  9. Uncover, and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes.
  10. Serve warm with crusty garlic bread and a green salad.
